## Increment distribution rules

- Inc 1 is already done. The plan must include **19 new AI increments** (Inc 2 through Inc 20) so the project has **at least 20 AI increments total** (Inc 1 + Inc 2–20).
- Group 1 (Andre + Anvik): 4 increments — Inc 2–5 — foundation work (lint toolchain, Wallet unit tests, refactor `src/main.js` into decoupled `src/controller/GameManager.js` + `src/ui/view.js`, GameManager unit tests). The UI skeleton already exists, so Group 1 does NOT build it — they clean up and decouple what's there.
- Group 2 (Jad + Yezhi + Noah): 5 increments — Inc 6–10 — visual/UX polish and README polish.
- Group 3 (Iban + Abas + Cadie): 5 increments — Inc 11–15 — bet/auto-spin/retention features and an AI self-audit pass.
- Group 4 (Christine + Adam + Esha): 5 increments — Inc 16–20 — Playwright E2E, RTP simulation, full lint sweep, acting on the self-audit findings, and one final hardening increment (suggested: AI-driven JSDoc/dead-code audit across `src/`, or generation of a seeded-RNG demo scenario script for video capture — pick the one that best supports the team deliverables without becoming one).

- The final report (`final-report/FINAL-REPORT.md`), slide deck, and presentation video are **NOT AI increments**. They are team deliverables completed collaboratively after Inc 20 — list them only in the "Team deliverables" section, and do not number them.

## Design constraints to reflect in the increments

- Strict 3-layer separation: Brain (`src/logic/`) and Wallet (`src/state/`) must stay DOM-free. The controller lives in `src/controller/GameManager.js`. DOM glue lives in `src/ui/`.
- No dark patterns: near-miss amplification, urgency timers, and unconditional autoplay are off-limits. Auto-spin must expose stop conditions.
- Must include "for amusement only / no real-money prizes / 21+" legal footer and the California problem-gambling hotline (persona "Bobby 9" + CA rules from research).
- Reel animation plays back an already-determined outcome (mirrors real-machine architecture).
