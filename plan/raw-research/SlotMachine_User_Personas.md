# User Focused Activities
## Persona 1:

Name: Marcus

Details: A 42 year old who regularly uses casino\-style apps to relax after work\. He is familiar with gambling style games such as sports betting and slot machines, and typically plays on his phone during downtime\. He enjoys the feeling of a real casino experience and is comfortable engaging with real money gambling features\.

Wants &amp; Needs: Wants clear and rewarding gameplay with stimulating features like satisfying win animations, sounds, and occasional bonuses\. He prefers games that are easy to understand and straightforward to use, without requiring too much effort to learn\. He also values the option for real money gambling, as it adds excitement and authenticity to the experience\. Overall, he is looking for a smooth, engaging, and realistic slot machine experience\.

Doesn&#39;t Want&#x2F;Need: Does not want mini games or overly complex systems that distract from the core slot experience\. He dislikes confusing user interfaces, unclear mechanics, or anything that feels misleading, such as hidden fees or unclear payouts\. If the game becomes too complicated or cluttered, he is likely to stop playing\.
