# UI/UX Design Research

### Layout & Interaction Hierarchy

- **Top Bar:** Balance, Settings, Menu.
- **Center (Focus):** 3x3 Reel area.
- **Bottom Bar:** Primary Spin button, Bet controls (+/-), Auto-spin toggle.
- **Hierarchy:** Spin is Primary; Bet is Secondary; Settings are Tertiary.
- **Mobile First:** Portrait-first, bottom-heavy controls, and large touch targets.

### Visual Identity

- **Palette:** High saturation (reds, golds, purples). Use glow effects and high-contrast gradients.
- **Theme:** Symbols and backgrounds must be consistent with the chosen theme.

### Animation & Feedback (The Loop)

- **Reel Motion:** Easing, smooth spin, and "bounce" stops.
- **Win Feedback (Exaggerated):** Coin bursts, flashing paylines, particle effects, and increasing audio intensity.
- **Loss Feedback (Subtle):** Fast reset to keep the "one-tap spin loop" moving.

### Wireframe Priorities

- Direct mapping of interaction flow.
- Clarity over decoration—fast readability is key.
- Optimize for repeated use and loop efficiency.
